About Me

I am a second-year PhD student in the School of Data Science at Fudan University. Before transferring to the PhD program, I spent two years in the MS program. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zengfeng Huang. I obtained my B.S. degree in Statistics from Donghua University in 2022.

I previously interned at Ant Group and Lilith Games. I am open to research collaboration with both academia and industry. If you are interested, please contact me via email.
